Thriller (Album):

The dynamic duo of Jackson and Jones began working on Thriller in April 1982 at the Westlake Audio studio in Los Angeles. Inspired by Tchaikovsky’s 'Nutcracker Suite', where Michael felt every song is a killer, his aim was to make Thriller an album where every song could be released as a single.

With a production budget of $750,000, the album session was completed in approximately seven months, and out of many demos that were produced, nine songs made the final cut.

Thriller is a mix of R&B, pop, rock, funk and ballads that has a little something for everyone’s taste and no B-sides.
