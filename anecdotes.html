Legendary guitarist Eddie Van Halen played the guitar solo on 'Beat It' for free!

Jackson performed Billie Jean live (and his signature moonwalk) for the first time at the 'Motown 25' showcase. He wore his mother's black sequin jacket which would go on to become part of his inconic outfits

Deborah Nadoolman Landis, designed the jacket used in the 'Thriller' music video. She also designed Indiana Jones' fedora and jacket look!

Rock group Toto known for their hits such as 'Africa' and 'Hold The Line' were quite involved in the Thriller project. The band's Steve Porcaro co-wrote 'Human Nature' while Steve Lukather, Jeff Porcaro and Steve Porcaro played in 'Beat It'.

The album took home a record-breaking number of Grammys in 1984: 8 in total, including Album of the Year and Best Pop Vocal Performance, Male.
